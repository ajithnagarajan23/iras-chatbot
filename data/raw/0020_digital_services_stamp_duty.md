The e-stamping Portal is integrated with [myTax Portal](https://mytax.iras.gov.sg/). To log in, select "Stamp Duty" and log in as "Individual User" or "Business User".

For business users who are stamping their documents, you must register for a Stamp Duty Account ID (SDID) via myTax Portal (Stamp Duty Login). To register for a new SDID, please assign "Corporate" role through Corppass to access the portal. An SDID will be created when you have successfully registered for your corporate Stamp Duty Account.

For individual users to perform stamping, please log in via Singpass. No SDID is required.

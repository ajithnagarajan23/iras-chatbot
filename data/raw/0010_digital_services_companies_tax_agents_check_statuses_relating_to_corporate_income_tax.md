<!-- title: Check Corporate Income Tax Statuses -->

Our IRAS Bot can assist companies in checking the following statuses. Simply click on the “Chat with IRAS” icon at the bottom right of the screen and select the relevant options.

**Form C-S/ Form C-S (Lite)/ Form C (for the current and 3 previous Years of Assessment):**

- Filing Status
- Tax Assessment Status

**Applications/ Submissions:**

- Revise/ Object to Assessment
- Application for strike-off
- Certificate/ Letter of Residence
- Declaration of Receipts and Payments for Companies under Liquidation

**Corporate Income Tax Payment:**

- GIRO Status
- Payment Status

For our IRAS Bot to assist with your status check, please have the company’s tax reference number\* ready.

_\\* The company's tax reference number is its Unique Entity Number (UEN), which falls within the categories below:_

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| Local Companies with UEN | E.g. 200312345A |
| Foreign Companies with UEN | E.g. T08FC1234A |

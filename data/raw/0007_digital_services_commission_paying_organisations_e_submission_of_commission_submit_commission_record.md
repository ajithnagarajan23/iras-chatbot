IRAS provides convenient and efficient ways  for commission-paying organisations to submit commission records to IRAS both electronically and digitally.

## Prepare and submit commission records

Organisations may use the following modes to submit the commission records to IRAS.

### Submit with customised software

| Submission mode | Description | Software/ guides |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Submission mode**'Submission of Commission Records (Corppass)' with Application Programming Interface (API) service | **Description**<br>This API service provides commission-paying organisations with a convenient and efficient way of submitting records to IRAS directly through their software.<br>It is suitable for organisations who:<br>- Use software to manage their commission income information, and<br>- Wish to submit their records to IRAS directly from their software. | **Software/ guides**<br>[API Specification](https://www.iras.gov.sg/docs/default-source/uploadedfiles/pdf/iras-submission-of-commission-records-(corppass)-api-services-interface-specification.pdf?sfvrsn=814fc2a7_11) (PDF, 284KB)<br>[Submission of Commission Records (Corppass) Sandbox Testing Guide](https://www.iras.gov.sg/docs/default-source/uploadedfiles/pdf/commission-api-cp-sandbox-testing-guide.pdf?sfvrsn=c447f50f_16) (PDF, 225KB) |

### Submit without customised software

| Submission mode | Description | Software/ guides |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Submission mode** <br>Submit Commission Records digital service at myTax Portal (online application) | **Description**<br>The online application is a digital service allowing organisations to submit their commission earners' information via myTax Portal.<br>It is suitable for organisations who:<br>- Do not use software to manage their commission income information; and<br>- Has less than 500 commission earners' records for each submission. | **Software/ guides**<br>**Software**<br>Log in to [myTax Portal](https://mytax.iras.gov.sg/ESVWeb/default.aspx "myTax Portal"), under the 'More' tab, select 'Submit Commission Records'.<br>**Guide**<br>[Submit Commission Records at myTax Portal (Online Application)](https://www.iras.gov.sg/docs/default-source/uploadedfiles/pdf/submit-commission-records-user-guide.pdf?sfvrsn=32548afc_35 "Guide on Submit Commission Records at myTax Portal (Online Application)") (PDF, 1.61MB)<br>[File Format - Import Excel](https://www.iras.gov.sg/docs/default-source/uploadedfiles/pdf/file-format-for-import-excel.pdf?sfvrsn=fb03b2c6_20 "File Format - Import Excel") (PDF, 198KB) |

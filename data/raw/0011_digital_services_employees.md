**Need help in using myTax portal?** Browse the [myTax Portal Technical FAQ](https://www.iras.gov.sg/digital-services/others/mytax-portal-technical-faq "myTax Portal Technical FAQ").

You need **Singpass 2-Step Verification (Singpass 2FA)** to log in to [mytax.iras.gov.sg](https://mytax.iras.gov.sg/login/selection). This means that you have to have to use the Singpass mobile app, select the face verification option or enter a One-Time Password
(OTP) sent via SMS to your mobile phone as a second level of authentication. Please register and activate your Singpass 2FA at [www.singpass.gov.sg](https://www.singpass.gov.sg/main).

**Need help in using myTax Portal?** Browse the [myTax Portal Technical FAQ](https://www.iras.gov.sg/digital-services/others/mytax-portal-technical-faq "myTax Portal Technical FAQ").

You need **Singpass 2-Step Verification (Singpass 2FA)** to log in to [myTax Portal](https://mytax.iras.gov.sg/login/selection "IRAS' myTax Portal"). This means that you have
to use the Singpass mobile app, face verification option or enter a One-Time Password (OTP) sent via SMS to your mobile phone as a second level of authentication. Please register and activate your Singpass 2FA at [www.singpass.gov.sg](https://www.singpass.gov.sg/main).

If you have authorised a tax agent to access your myTax Portal account via the ' [Manage Tax Agent Authorisation](https://www.iras.gov.sg/digital-services/self-employed#MTAA "Manage Tax Agent Authorisation")' digital service (or previously via an IRAS-issued Authorisation Code), you will be deemed as having authorised your tax agent to:

- Handle your Individual Income Tax matters;
- Correspond with IRAS on your Individual Income Tax matters through any mode of communication including telephone calls, myTax Mails, emails, hardcopy letters and in-person meetings.
